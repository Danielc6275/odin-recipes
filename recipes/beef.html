Beef

Description

Someone is about to have some beef, literally :D. Ground beef is used in many meals like tacos, burritos and even used just by itself. Definitely recommended with rice, tomatoes, lettuce, and more.

Ingredients

  • Beef cube
  • Olive oil

Steps

  1. Spray a pan with spray oil or olive oil
  2. Pour frozen beef cube into pan
  3. Cook until brown
