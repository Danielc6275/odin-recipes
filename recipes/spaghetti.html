Spaghetti

Description

Spaghetti is a classic italian dish that can be made right from home. Topped with olive oil, pasta sauce, spinach, or any topping you can think of, it is a fancy dinner in your own home. It can come in both regular and whole-wheat versions.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 boxes of spaghetti
  • Olive oil
  • Pasta Saue
  • Water
  • Toppings (pepper, spinach, etc)

Steps

  1. Heat a large pot of water until boiling
  2. Pour spaghetti into pot
  3. Stir in boiling water for 10-20 minutes
  4. Empty contents into strainer
  5. Enjoy!!!
