Brownies

Description

This ultra-chocolate fudge nut brownie is created by first mixing brownie mix, butter, water, peanut butter (1/2 cup) and milk (1/2 tbsp) in a bowl. Then heat the oven to 350 degrees, and place the concoction into the oven once at 350. Cook for 27-29 minutes tops and afterward, take the brownies out and let them cool for about 20 minutes. Finally, enjoy with a glass of milk ;)

Ingredients

  • Packet of brownie mix
  • Water (1/2 cup)
  • Butter (1/2 cup)
  • Peanut Butter (1/2 cup)
  • Milk (1/2 tbsp)

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 350 *F
  2. Mix all the ingredients together
  3. Place in oven
  4. Cook for about 27-29 minutes
  5. Take the brownies out of the oven
  6. Let the brownies sit for 20 minutes
  7. Add chocolate chips and enjoy!
